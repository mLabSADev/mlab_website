<p>Join us for two days of intense focus on how to build a robust and flexible Financial Model fit for Angel Investment &#8211; capped with a 1:1 session with Newtown Partners at the end.</p>
<p>What each attendee will get out:</p>
<ul>
<li>Detailed Financial Model spreadsheet</li>
<li>Business Model Canvas best practices</li>
<li>Business Metrics &amp; Assumptions to test</li>
<li>A Staffing and Growth funding plan</li>
<li>Proven Cost-Effective Sales Pipeline Tools</li>
<li>Funding Pitch Deck Framework and tips</li>
<li>Running an investment funding process</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>When: 7th â€“ 8th October 2015</strong></p>
<p><strong>Where: mLab @ Workshop17, V&amp;A Waterfront, Cape Town.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Who: Technology startups with an existing or new product (MVP) who are ready to create or improve on their Financial Model and seek investment.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><em>ABOUT: Justin Coetzee is the Founder and CEO of GoMetro. GoMetro is an alumni of the mLab. In 2014, GoMetro was runner-up at the Final of the GEW Get in the Ring Global Startup Competition in Rotterdam, NL.</em></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><em>In 2014, GoMetro raised significant seed investment from AngelHub Ventures and 4Decades Capital and has grown revenues, team and users in the last 18 months.</em></p>
