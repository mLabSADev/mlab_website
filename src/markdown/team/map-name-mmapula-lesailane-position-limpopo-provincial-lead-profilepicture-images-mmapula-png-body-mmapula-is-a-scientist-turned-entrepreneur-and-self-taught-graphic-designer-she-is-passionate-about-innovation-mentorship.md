Mmapula is a scientist-turned-entrepreneur and self-taught graphic designer, she is passionate about innovation, mentorship, and creating opportunities that empower people and communities.

She finds great fulfilment in supporting the growth of individuals and businesses, and considers one of her proudest achievements to be seeing the people and companies she has mentored succeed across the world. Helping others grow and reach their potential is what motivates her most.

Outside of work, she enjoys hiking, landscaping, and spending time outdoors creating beautiful spaces that inspire creativity and balance.
