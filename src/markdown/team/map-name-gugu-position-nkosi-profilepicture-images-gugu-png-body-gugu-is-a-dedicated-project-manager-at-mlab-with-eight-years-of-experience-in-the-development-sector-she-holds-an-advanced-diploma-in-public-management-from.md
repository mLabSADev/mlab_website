Gugu is a dedicated Project Manager at mLab with eight years of experience in the development sector. She holds an Advanced Diploma in Public Management from the Durban University of Technology and is passionate about driving projects that create meaningful impact and empower communities.

With strong experience in project coordination and stakeholder engagement, Gugu is committed to supporting initiatives that contribute to skills development and social innovation. She brings a people-centred approach to her work and enjoys collaborating with teams to deliver impactful programmes.

Outside of work, Gugu enjoys hiking and spending time in nature. September is her favourite time of year, especially for long walks in Pretoria when the purple jacaranda trees are in bloom.
