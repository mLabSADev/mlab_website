Mahlatse is a Digital Skills Facilitator in Limpopo who is passionate about technology, mentorship, and youth development. A proud milestone in her journey was graduating as part of the 2023 CodeTribe cohort, which marked an important step in her personal and professional growth.

She later became an intern and facilitator for the 2024/2025 CodeTribe programme, where she supported 11 trainees throughout their learning journey. Seeing the trainees graduate with fully developed mobile applications was a rewarding experience that strengthened her passion for mentoring others and showcased her ability to lead and inspire teamwork in both creative and technical environments.

Outside of work, she is a video game enthusiast and a proud supporter of Liverpool F.C.She also enjoys attending stand-up comedy shows and spending time laughing with friends and family.
